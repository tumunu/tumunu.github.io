How it works:
1. Capture: You (the client) use the Tumunu Forensic Imager to create an encrypted disk image.
We provide a downloadable, court-compliant tool and simple instructions. Your data never leaves your hands until encrypted and logged.
2. Submit: For small to medium files, upload your encrypted evidence and chain-of-custody log using our one-time secure upload link.
For large disk images, send the encrypted device to Tumunu via your preferred tracked courier.
For best practice, use tamper-evident packaging and keep a copy of the chain-of-custody record for your files. Full instructions and templates are provided. You are responsible for secure delivery; Tumunu will confirm receipt and note any issues.
3. Analysis: Tumunu decrypts and analyzes your evidence in a dedicated forensic lab. Every step is documented for legal defensibility.
4. Results: We provide a detailed report (PDF), summary findings, and expert support. All evidence is preserved per your instructions.
