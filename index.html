How Tumunu Works

  1. Capture: Use the Tumunu open source forensic imager to create secure disk images.
    Download our Rust-based forensic platform. Enterprise-grade performance with 48-82% speed improvements. No cloud dependency for imaging.
  2. Submit: Upload your encrypted evidence and chain-of-custody log via secure channels, or courier the encrypted device with tamper-evident packaging.
  3. Analysis: Tumunu's forensic experts analyze your evidence in our dedicated lab. Every step is documented for legal defensibility.
  4. Results: Receive detailed reports, expert testimony support, and professional consultation. Evidence is preserved per your instructions.

FAQ

Where can I find pricing for analysis services?
Pricing is only visible inside the Tumunu CLI tool itself using the `--pricing-details` flag.
Why? Restricting pricing info to the CLI, not the website, is a deliberate security measure.
It minimizes our web attack surface, reduces automated scraping, and signals our security-first approach to all clients and adversaries.
We believe that digital forensics providers should minimize publicly exposed business logic and sensitive information.

Can I use the tools without paying for analysis?
Yes. All imaging and verification tools are open source/ You only pay for professional analysis services.

Why is this site minimalist?
Tumunu is for digital forensics, not marketing. Minimalism removes distractions, avoids exposing unnecessary metadata, and reduces risk. All core details are here; everything else is handled via our CLI or directly with clients.
