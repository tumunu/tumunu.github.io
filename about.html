What this is

Tumunu is a small collective that forms project-specific teams across Wellington and Rarotonga.
We are not a single large company; we operate through named individuals/entities per engagement, stated in writing before work begins.

Governance / contracting clarity

  • Accountability: Every project has a named lead responsible for delivery and communication.
  • Scope control: We document scope and constraints before committing. Variations are explicit.
  • Contracting: The contracting party (individual or entity) is named per project.
  • Verification: Credentials and evidence are provided on request (where appropriate).
  • Fit: We decline work that can't be delivered to a professional standard.
